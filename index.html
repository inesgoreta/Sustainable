Sustainable Website

The climate crisis makes it necessary for us to think differently about our energy consumption.
We also have to do that in our digital everyday life.

The Problem

Sustainable websites can consume a lot of energy if they are not designed and hosted with environmentally friendly practices in mind. This can be a problem for the environment as well as for businesses that want to minimize their carbon footprint.

Solution

You can therefore as a multimediedesigner help the envoirment by creating a sustainable website.

1.
The internet and digital technology caused 3.7% of global carbon emissions in 2018

2.
The research predicted that the 3.7% from 2018 will double by 2025

3.
This is why we need eco-friendly websites.

Characteristics of a sustainable website

1. Clean
“The services we provide and services we use will be powered by renewable energy.”

2. Efficient
“The products and services we provide will use the least amount of energy and material resources possible.”

3. Open
“The products and services we provide will be accessible, allow for the open exchange of information, and allow users to control their data.”

4. Honest
“The products and services we provide will not mislead or exploit users in their design or content.”

5. Regenerative
“The products and services we provide will support an economy that nourishes people and planet.”

6. Resilient
“The products and services we provide will function in the times and places where people need them most.”
